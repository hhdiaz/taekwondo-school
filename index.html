About Us

Cobas Taekwondo is a school built on tradition and excellence in teaching the Korean martial art. We combine traditional methods with modern teaching to offer the best training. We are located at 2510 W 78th St, Hialeah, FL 33016

Our mission is to develop not only physical skills, but also values such as discipline, respect and perseverance that will accompany our students throughout their lives.

We have certified instructors and follow the official belt and rank program, ensuring internationally recognized training quality.

Master José Ángel Cobas del Valle

2º Dan Kukkiwon Certified

Master José Ángel Cobas del Valle, born in Havana in 1994, is one of the greatest figures in Cuban taekwondo. At just 16 years old he made history by becoming the first Cuban to win a medal at a Junior World Championship (silver in 2010) and qualify for the Youth Olympic Games.

His crowning achievement came at the 2015 Pan American Games in Toronto, where he won the gold medal in the -80 kg category. This title is complemented by the bronze in Lima 2019 and two consecutive championships at the Central American and Caribbean Games (2014 and 2018), establishing himself as a continental reference for over a decade.

"My philosophy has always been total commitment in every fight and every training session. Taekwondo demands a titanic level of dedication, and that effort is what builds character and leads to success, inside and outside the ring."

Throughout his career he accumulated victories in prestigious international tournaments and trained alongside Olympic and World champions, forging a comprehensive vision that combines high-performance technique with the traditional philosophy of taekwondo.

Today, Master Cobas channels all his experience into training new generations, passing on not only a champion's technique, but the values of discipline, respect and perseverance that define this martial art as a true way of life.

Frequently Asked Questions

At what age can children start?

+

Children can start from age 4. Our kids' classes are designed to develop basic motor skills, coordination and discipline in a fun and safe way.

Do I need prior experience?

+

No prior experience is required. Our programs are designed to adapt to all levels, from beginners to advanced. Each student progresses at their own pace.

What equipment do I need to get started?

+

You only need comfortable athletic clothing. The uniform (dobok) and protective gear can be purchased at the school after your first class. We will advise you on the right equipment for your level.

Do you offer trial classes?

+

Yes, we offer a free trial class so you can meet our facilities, instructors and teaching method before signing up. You can schedule it through our contact form.

Do you hold belt exams?

+

Yes, we hold grading exams every 3-4 months, following the standards of the World Taekwondo Federation. Exams assess technique, form, sparring and theoretical knowledge.
